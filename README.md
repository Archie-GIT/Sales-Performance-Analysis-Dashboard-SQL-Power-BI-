# Sales-Performance-Analysis-Dashboard-SQL-Power-BI-

## 1. Short Description / Purpose

Performed sales data exploration and aggregation using SQL to uncover insights on product demand, territory performance, and revenue drivers. Designed a dynamic Microsoft Power BI dashboard with KPIs and visualizations to support data-driven sales analysis.

This dashboard enables business analysts, managers, and decision-makers to monitor key sales metrics and make data-driven strategic decisions.

---

# 2. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 **Microsoft Power BI Desktop** – Used to design the interactive dashboard and visualizations.

• 🗄 **SQL** – Used for querying the dataset, performing aggregations, and extracting business insights.

• 📂 **Power Query** – Used for cleaning, transforming, and preparing the dataset before visualization.

• 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures such as Total Revenue, Total Orders, and Year-over-Year growth.

• 🧩 **Data Modeling** – Relationships created between tables such as Products, Orders, and Territories to enable filtering and aggregation.

• 📁 **File Formats**

* `.sql` – SQL queries used for data analysis
* `.pbix` – Power BI dashboard file
* `.png` – Dashboard preview images

# 3. Data Source

**Source:** Sales transactional dataset.

The dataset contains sales transaction data across multiple territories and product lines.

### Key Fields in the Dataset

* Order Number
* Order Date
* Customer Name
* Product Line
* Product Name
* Quantity Ordered
* Sales Revenue
* Territory / Region
* Country

The dataset enables analysis of **sales performance, product demand, and regional revenue distribution**.

---

# 4. Features / Highlights

## Business Problem

Businesses generate large volumes of sales transaction data, making it difficult to quickly identify revenue trends, product performance, and regional sales patterns.

Key questions often faced by business stakeholders include:

* Which product lines generate the most revenue?
* Which territories drive the highest sales?
* How do sales trends change over time?
* Which products contribute most to business growth?

Without structured analytics, these insights are difficult to extract from raw transactional data.

---

## Goal of the Dashboard

The goal of this dashboard is to provide an **interactive sales analytics tool** that:
* Tracks key sales performance indicators
* Identifies top-performing products and regions
* Visualizes revenue trends over time
* Enables users to explore sales data through interactive filters

---

# Walkthrough of Key Visuals

### KPI Cards

Displays important business metrics such as:
* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Total Product Lines

These KPIs provide a quick summary of overall sales performance.

---

### Revenue by Product Line (Bar Chart)
Shows how different product categories contribute to overall revenue.
This helps identify the most profitable product segments.

---

### Sales by Territory (Bar Chart)
Compares revenue generated across different geographic territories, highlighting regional performance differences.

---

### Sales Trend Over Time (Line Chart)
Displays monthly or yearly sales trends, helping identify growth patterns and seasonal demand.

---

### Top Products by Revenue (Bar Chart)
Ranks the best-performing products based on revenue contribution.

---

### Product Line Distribution (Pie / Donut Chart)

Shows the proportion of revenue generated by different product categories.
